Vene Game - Camera: follow - Speed: normal

Movement: WASD

Jump: SPACE

Crouch: C

Speed Controls:

  • Normal Speed: Default (7.5 units/s)
  • Fast Speed: Hold SHIFT (15 units/s)
  • Slow Speed: Hold ALT (2.5 units/s)

Camera: Hold LEFT MOUSE BUTTON and move mouse

Zoom: Mouse Wheel

Toggle Camera Mode: T (follow/orbit)
